General Information

We keep track of how often you are using our app in order to best understand your water drinking habits as well as how to improve our app features to make your experience better. The only other data we keep track of is the information you give us based on how often you are drinking water. We also keep track of crash data which allows us to improve bug fixes that will improve your experience using the application.

Information Gathering and Usage

Drink Up! collects anonymous usage information. It also collects general location information for anonymous usage (for example, "user in Ontario, Canada added a drink"). We collect this data to find what features you are using and what we can improve.

Changes

Drink Up! may occasionally update this policy. If you've shared your contact information with us, we'll notify you about significant changes to the way we treat your personal information.

Have a comment?

If you have questions or comments about our privacy policy please fill out the feedback form which can be found in-app settings.
