Welcome to Drink Up!

The Drink Up! Team wants to encourage users to live a healthier lifestyle starting with one of the most important foundations in healthy living - drinking water.

Drink Up! is a water-tracking app designed to encourage users to drink more water. The app allows you to set a daily water goal and throughout your day will remind you to drink water. You can adjust your goal based on whether or not you are actively training or working out, or have the app generate a goal for your based on your age and other health factors.
